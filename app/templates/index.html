Ensuring Fair Play

Referees make split-second decisions, but with TrueVAR's Instant Video Replay, every critical moment is captured, reviewed, and verified in real-time. No more missed points or unfair calls—justice is just a click away.

Easy to use

Designed for referees, coaches, and event organizers, TrueVAR features a simple and intuitive control panel. Quick access to replays, easy navigation between matches, and an efficient review system make decision-making effortless.

Capture Every Angle

With support for up to 4 camera feeds, referees can switch perspectives instantly, ensuring no action goes unseen. Side, overhead, close-up—every crucial moment is covered from the best possible angle.

Seamless Livestream

Expand your audience with smooth, high-quality livestreaming, with integrated scoreboard. TrueVAR allows real-time video feed integration, ensuring fans, coaches, and families can follow the action without delay.

From Local to International

Whether you're organizing a small club competition or a world-class championship. TrueVAR adapts to your needs. With scalable features and flexible setup options, it’s built to handle tournaments of any size, anywhere.
